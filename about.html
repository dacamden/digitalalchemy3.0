About

Digital Alchemy is a team taking part in Founders and Coders II

Founders and Coders is an intensive full-time course in web development. Run by course Director Dan Sofer the aim is to give students sufficient skills to seek freelance projects, to find employment, or to work on their own web-based startup.
There are no fees, but students are expected to engage in a range of activities in support of both the the course and their own vocational development, including communicating with potential sponsors, employers and clients, and working on client projects.
Digital Alchemy is a team of 4 students on the Founders and Coders course. Find out more about each of us below.
